Information about Ask Me Discord bot commands.

!I - prints information about available commands
!Q text - Answers Your question.
!F text - Have a friendly chat.
!S text - Chat with Marv. Marv is a factual chatbot that is also sarcastic.
!R meal ingredient1 ingredient2 ... - Create a recipe from meal name and  a list of ingredients.
!T topic - Provide a topic and get study notes.
